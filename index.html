Our Story

Every Flavors Tells a Story

Six friends from different regions were brought together in Depok. They had the same dream, to serve delicious and affordable authentic Palembang pempek to the people of Indonesia. With hard work and determination, they managed to realize their dream. In 2023, PPmpek Kito has become one of the most popular pempek brands in Indonesia.

PEMPEK KITO


At Pempek Kito, we aim to offer high-quality, delicious pempek made from the freshest ingredients. Our team is dedicated to maintaining hygiene standards and ensuring customer satisfaction with every bite.

We are committed to continuous improvement and innovation to keep our offerings fresh and exciting. Pempek Kito aspires to become a leading brand in traditional Indonesian cuisine, recognized for its quality and taste.

Through our journey with Pempek Kito, we have learned the importance of creativity and innovation in the culinary business. We have also honed our skills in managing various aspects of the business, including finance, marketing, and human resources.
